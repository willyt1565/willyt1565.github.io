With deep roots in the Houston market and expanding throughout Texas, About Newera

Built by Investors, For Investors.

Newera isn't a legacy firm looking for more doors. We're a modern property management company built around one idea — that owners deserve the same clarity, accountability, and ROI-focused thinking that professional investors demand from every asset in their portfolio.

Our Story

A New Era of Property Management in Houston

Newera Property Management wasn't born in a corporate office — it was born from frustration. Our founders have spent years in the trenches: buying distressed properties, renovating them, managing rehabilitation timelines and contractor relationships, and learning what it takes to keep a property profitable and well-maintained. As our fix-and-flip portfolio grew into rental holdings, we realized that most property management companies don't think the way investors think. They treat properties like files, not assets. They optimize for their commission, not your long-term wealth. So we built Newera as the property management company we wished existed when we started out.

Because we own and actively manage properties ourselves, we bring something most traditional property managers lack: real investor perspective. We understand renovation economics. We know which repairs protect property value and which ones are unnecessary. We recognize that responsive maintenance isn't an expense — it's an investment in asset protection. Every decision we make is informed by our own property holdings. We don't just manage your investments; we manage them the way we'd manage our own.

Today, Newera combines our decade of hands-on real estate experience with modern property management technology. We're a licensed Texas broker focused exclusively on Houston single-family and small multifamily properties — the same properties we invest in ourselves. That alignment means your success is our success.

Our Mission

Property management should be as modern, transparent, and accountable as the investments it protects.

We bring that standard to every home, every owner, and every resident we serve.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Four principles that shape how we run every property, every interaction, and every report.

Transparency

We believe property owners deserve complete visibility into their investments. Every action is documented, every report is clear, and every decision is explained. No hidden fees, no surprises — just honest, straightforward communication.

Accountability

We take ownership of every property, every issue, and every dollar that passes through our hands. When something goes wrong, we fix it. When we make a commitment, we keep it. Your success is our responsibility.

Investor Focus

We're not just managing homes — we're protecting and growing assets. Every decision prioritizes your property's long-term value, cash flow, and profitability. We think like owners because we are owners.

Modern Excellence

Technology should serve people, not complicate things. We combine cutting-edge property management tools with responsive human support, giving you the best of both worlds — convenience, speed, and a real person when you need one.
