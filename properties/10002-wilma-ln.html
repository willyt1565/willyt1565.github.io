Launching soon — fall in love with this 2024-built farmhouse-style home in the heart of Beach City. Sit on the wraparound covered front porch and take in views of McCollum Park and Trinity Bay just across the way. The shaded park features walking paths, a playground, picnic areas, a kayak/wade-fishing ramp, and a covered pavilion — with a planned expansion adding a boat ramp and sports fields.

Inside, an open-concept living, dining, and kitchen anchors the first floor. The kitchen is the show-stopper: quartz countertops, gray shaker cabinets, stainless appliances with a gas range and chimney hood, oversized island with breakfast-bar seating, and a designer chandelier. Engineered hardwood flows through the main living areas, with crown molding and recessed lighting throughout.

Five total bedrooms across two stories. The first floor includes the primary suite with en-suite bath plus a second bedroom and full bath. Upstairs you'll find three more bedrooms — one of which works perfectly as an office or game room — plus a third full bathroom. Out back, a fully fenced yard with a double gate leaves room to add a shop or detached garage. New construction means low-emissivity windows, efficient HVAC, and zero deferred maintenance.
